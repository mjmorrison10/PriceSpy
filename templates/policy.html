📋 Overview

PriceSpy is a price research tool designed for resellers and flippers. We use the eBay Browse API to provide real-time pricing data including sold listings, active listings, fees, and liquidity metrics.

This policy explains what data PriceSpy accesses from eBay, what user information we store, and how we comply with eBay's developer requirements for privacy and data handling.

eBay Compliance: PriceSpy is committed to transparency about data access and handling in accordance with eBay's API Terms of Use and Developer Program policies.

🔍 Data Access from eBay

When you use PriceSpy, we access the following information from eBay's API:

  • Public Listing Data: Item titles, descriptions, prices, conditions, and images from eBay listings
  • Sold Listing Information: Historical sales data including final prices, sale dates, and item conditions
  • Pricing Metrics: Market values, price ranges, and pricing trends based on aggregated listing data
  • Category Information: Product categories and item specifics to improve search accuracy
  • Liquidity Data: Average days to sell, sales velocity, and competition metrics

We do not access your eBay account information, login credentials, personal messages, or any private user data from eBay.

Public Data Only: PriceSpy only accesses publicly available listing information from eBay. We do not store your eBay username, password, or any authentication credentials.

👤 User Data We Store

PriceSpy stores the following user information:

  • Email Address: Used for account creation and authentication
  • Search History: Your recent searches to provide personalized recommendations and faster access to previous queries
  • Watchlists: Items you're tracking for price changes and market analysis
  • Inventory Lists: Items you're tracking for your resale business
  • Account Settings: Preferences and configuration options
No eBay Account Details: We do not collect or store your eBay username, eBay password, eBay authentication tokens, or any eBay account-specific information.

🔒 How We Handle Your Data

PriceSpy takes data security seriously:

  • Encryption: All data is transmitted over HTTPS with TLS encryption
  • Secure Storage: User credentials and sensitive data are hashed and stored securely
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties
  • Limited Access: Only authorized personnel have access to user data, and only when necessary for service operation
  • Data Retention: We retain your data only as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services

🗑️ Account & Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your PriceSpy account and all associated data, we provide the following options:

Request Account Deletion

To request complete deletion of your PriceSpy account and all stored data:

Email: Contact us at mjmorrisonusa@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request"

Please include the email address associated with your PriceSpy account.

eBay Marketplace Account Deletion

If you have linked your eBay account or have concerns about data shared through eBay's APIs, you can manage your eBay data preferences directly through eBay's platform.

For questions about how eBay handles your data, please visit eBay's Privacy Center.

eBay Webhook Compliance: PriceSpy maintains a webhook endpoint at /ebay/account-deletion to receive account deletion notifications from eBay, as required by their Developer Program policies. This endpoint only processes eBay's official notifications and does not affect user account data stored in PriceSpy.
